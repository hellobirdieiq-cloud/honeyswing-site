HoneySwing Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

HoneySwing may collect basic account information, swing analysis results, and practice history to provide coaching feedback.

Video Storage

Swing videos remain stored locally on the user's device and are not uploaded to HoneySwing servers.

AI Processing

A thumbnail image from a recorded swing may be sent to an AI service to generate coaching feedback.

Data Usage

Aggregated swing data may be used to improve HoneySwing's coaching system.
