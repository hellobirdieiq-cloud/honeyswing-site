Privacy Policy

Effective March 25, 2026

HoneySwing ("we," "our," "us") is a golf swing analysis app for junior golfers. We take your privacy seriously — especially because many of our users are children. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to sign you in via magic link (no passwords stored)
  • Display name — shown in the app to personalize your experience
  • Coach name — links you to your golf coach within the app
  • Handedness — left or right, so coaching feedback is accurate for your swing

When you use the app, we collect:

  • Swing performance data — scores, timing, tempo, and coaching metrics from your recorded swings

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect video. All video stays on your device.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not serve ads.

How We Use Your Data

All data we collect is used solely to make HoneySwing work — to authenticate your account, deliver coaching feedback, and track your progress over time. We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or any purpose beyond app functionality.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

HoneySwing is designed for junior golfers. We require parental consent for account creation for users under 13. Our parent signup process collects the parent's email address and requires the parent to create the account on behalf of their child.

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the app to function. A parent or guardian can request deletion of their child's account and data at any time by contacting us.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted storage and row-level security. Each user can only access their own data. We do not store passwords — authentication is handled via magic link email.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at hello@honeyswing.com.
