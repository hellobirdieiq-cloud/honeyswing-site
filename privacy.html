HoneySwing Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

HoneySwing may collect basic account information, swing analysis results, and practice history in order to provide coaching feedback and track improvement.

Video Storage

Swing videos are processed locally on the user's device and remain stored on the user's device unless the user chooses to export or share them.

AI Processing

A thumbnail image generated from a recorded swing may be securely sent to an AI service in order to generate coaching feedback and analysis.

AI Disclaimer

Coaching feedback generated by HoneySwing may include AI-assisted analysis and should be considered informational guidance only.

Data Usage

Aggregated and anonymized swing data may be used to improve HoneySwing's coaching and analysis systems.

Children's Privacy

HoneySwing is intended to be used with parental guidance for younger players. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental involvement.

Third-Party Services

HoneySwing uses third-party services such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, and RevenueCat for subscription management.
